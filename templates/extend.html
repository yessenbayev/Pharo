Our Lock

Convenient. Intuitive. Secure.

Pharo combines convience, simplicity and security into an easy attachable lock. With quick installation and complimentary app, Pharo is the smart lock of the future! Preorder today!

Intuitive App

Our conjunct app allows you to lock and unlock your bike with the press of a button, quick and efficent. No more wrestling with keys or locks!

GPS Tracking

For the extra security on your bike, our lock contains an embedded GPS Tracker to inform the bike's location at all times. We send notifications if the bike moves!

Easy to Install

With seamless installation, Pharo can be quickly attached without leaving a single scratch on your bike!
